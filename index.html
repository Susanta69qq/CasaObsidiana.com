connects us through the rare power of the volcano's ancient stone, the obsidian.

The Power of Obsidian

Obsidian, found in abundance in the volcanic earth of our estate agave fields, represents the
ancient ritual of cleansing oneself of negative energy. It is dark in color in order to create light
within us, revealing the desire to create positive connections and to appreciate all things
beautiful. Each bottle of Casa Obsidiana houses a pure obsidian relic, a perfect circle in divine
proportion representing wholeness, continuity, and the infinite nature of being.
